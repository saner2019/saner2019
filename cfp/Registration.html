Visa Assistance Letters

The organizing committee will provide visa letters to participants on demand. Please contact the Local Team at saner19@zju.edu.cn to get a visa letter. In your request, please specify in English your:

  1. Name
  2. Gender
  3. Nationality
  4. Date of Birth
  5. Passport Number
  6. The Chinese Embassy/Consulate General you are going to submit your application

Usually, a visa letter issued by SANER 2019 Organizing Committee is good for a Chinese visa. If you need an official letter for an "F" visa, please mention it in your request.
Please note that no visa letters will be issued unless one has already successfully registered for the conference.

Note to Authors

Each accepted paper must have at least one author registered at a professional member rate. Papers for which the only registered authors are students must have one of the students register at the full (i.e., non-student) rate
