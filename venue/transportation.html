The Dragon Hotel

Transportation:

The Dragon to Hangzhou Int’l Airport:

Bus+Airport shuttle Bus: 1~1.5 hour. Go out of the hotel and across the road, take Bus No. 318 to Wulin Little Square Station, then to Airport by Airport shuttle bus.(For there is no direct bus from the airport to the hotel, we recommend you to take a taxi. Please prepare the Chinese cash first in case the credit card is not supported, and the taxi fee is about 20 - 25 US dollars, or 130 - 160 Chinese yuan)

The Dragon to Hangzhou East Railway Station: 7.2Km

Bus+Subway: 40 minutes. Go out of the hotel and across the road, take Bus No. 28 to Wulin Square Station, then change to Subway No. 1 to East Railway Station.

The Dragon to Westlake: 1.8km

On foot: 20 minutes by walking distance. go out of the hotel, turn left, go straight to Baoshu Road, then turn right, then go straight.

By bus: 20 minutes by bus. Go out of the hotel and across the road, take Bus No. 28 (or No103、No49) to Qiantangmenwai Station

Public Transportation in Hangzhou:

The public transport in Hangzhou is quite convenient, if you want to take a taxi, ask the hotel reception for arrangement. And just in front of the gate of hotel, there is a bus station. The red circles are the nearest metro station. Moreover, Hangzhou has the best public bicycle system in China, and we also have lots of shared bicycle around the city, so we strongly recommend you to cycle around the west lake after meeting. We hope you would have a chance to enjoy experience that is uniquely Hangzhou during your visit here.
